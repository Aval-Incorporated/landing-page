About Aval

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Aval emerged from a simple recognition: modern business software—especially in marketing—has become unnecessarily complex and fragmented. Businesses, marketers, and individuals are often forced to rely on bloated tools and manual processes that slow execution and limit clarity.


Aval takes a different approach—building focused, automated software systems designed to operate with simplicity and precision. Each product is purpose-built to solve a specific problem, delivering intelligent automation and measurable outcomes without unnecessary complexity.
